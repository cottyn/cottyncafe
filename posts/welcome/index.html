Welcome

2024/11/22

Hello! You either found my site through one of my socials, or I shamelessly shilled it to you in excitement. Regardless, I’m thrilled you’re here!

This site took a while to get up and running. Mainly because of bouts of random demotivation, but also because setting up a website is much more work than I was expecting. In all honesty, I thought getting a cute domain would be the difficult part, turns out it was just 6 bucks. I’m honestly really impressed by people who manage to do this shit from scratch, it’s probably a nightmare.

But that aside, this site is mainly going to be used as a creative outlet for me. Something I can use to keep up with myself, and for those who care to keep up with me! I plan on posting about a bunch of different things, opinions, reviews, personal thoughts, progress on projects, etc., and I hope you enjoy keeping up to date with my shitty, incredibly uninteresting life.

Welcome to the café. <3
